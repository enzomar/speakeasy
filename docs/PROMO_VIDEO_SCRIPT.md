# SpeakEasy — AI Video Script

## Global Prompt Seed

Prepend this to every scene prompt:

> Calm modern product video for an AAC communication app on a realistic smartphone. Warm daylight, soft coral and mint UI accents, clean rounded mobile interface, inclusive adult user, minimal background clutter, gentle camera motion, oversized readable controls, premium brand film style, respectful human tone.

## Negative Prompt

> No distorted hands, no illegible tiny text, no sci-fi holograms, no cyberpunk style, no pity-based framing, no extra fingers, no dark lighting, no cluttered dashboards.

## Version A — 30-45 Second Promo (9:16)

**Scene 1 · 0:00–0:04**
An adult pauses mid-conversation, then a smartphone appears with a calm AAC interface, large rounded buttons, bright layout.
*Voiceover:* When speaking is difficult, every second matters.
*Caption:* Communication should feel easier.

**Scene 2 · 0:04–0:09**
Macro phone shot. Symbol buttons — water, please, help — appear one by one. Message bar fills with a short phrase. Natural finger taps.
*Voiceover:* Build messages with symbols, typing, and a calm simple interface.
*Caption:* Symbols that build real messages fast.

**Scene 3 · 0:09–0:14**
Speak button glows softly. "I need water, please" appears large on screen. Subtle sound-wave ripples from the speaker.
*Voiceover:* Tap once, and your message is spoken out loud.
*Caption:* Tap. Speak. Be understood.

**Scene 4 · 0:14–0:20**
Suggestion chips slide in below the message bar. Labels are large: "Can you help me", "I need a short break".
*Voiceover:* Smart suggestions help you respond faster.
*Caption:* Smart suggestions, right on device.

**Scene 5 · 0:20–0:27**
Listen Mode. Another person speaks in the background. The phone shows a calm listening state, then reply suggestions appear.
*Voiceover:* When someone speaks to you, Listen Mode suggests useful replies in the moment.
*Caption:* Listen Mode for real conversations.

**Scene 6 · 0:27–0:33**
Settings screen showing voice, typing, and listening as three clearly separated language controls. Large labels, no tiny menus.
*Voiceover:* Ten languages, with separate settings for voice, typing, and listening.
*Caption:* 10 languages. Separate voice and input settings.

**Scene 7 · 0:33–0:40**
Hero shot of the phone held in one hand. Bright calm interface. A small on-device shield or pulse near the phone — no cloud icon.
*Voiceover:* Everything runs on device, so communication stays private.
*Caption:* Private by default. Web, iPhone, and Android.

**Scene 8 · 0:40–0:45**
Clean end card. Phone centered on a soft gradient. Space at top and bottom for logo and CTA added in post.
*Voiceover:* SpeakEasy. A calmer, more private AAC experience.
*Caption:* Try SpeakEasy.

## Version B — 60-90 Second Demo (16:9 or 9:16)

**Scene 1 · 0:00–0:08**
Smartphone in portrait, clean AAC home screen, large category cards and message bar. Soft daylight, calm background.
*Voiceover:* This is SpeakEasy — an AAC app designed to make communication calmer, faster, and more private.

**Scene 2 · 0:08–0:18**
Hand taps symbol buttons, then a typed word completes the sentence. Both symbols and text visible together in the composer.
*Voiceover:* Build messages with symbols, typing, or both combined in the same sentence.

**Scene 3 · 0:18–0:28**
"I need water, please" fills the message bar. Matching symbols visible nearby. Large and readable.
*Voiceover:* A simple phrase for an everyday need.

**Scene 4 · 0:28–0:34**
Speak button pressed. Soft audio-wave animation. Conversation partner reacts calmly in soft focus.
*Voiceover:* Tap speak and the app reads the message out loud immediately.

**Scene 5 · 0:34–0:44**
Two or three suggestion chips animate in beneath the composer. Each label is large and readable.
*Voiceover:* SpeakEasy also suggests next phrases to reduce effort in frequent conversations.

**Scene 6 · 0:44–0:58**
Listen Mode. Calm listening state on screen, then contextual replies appear in a clean stack. Second person in soft focus.
*Voiceover:* Listen Mode captures context and suggests useful replies when someone speaks to you.

**Scene 7 · 0:58–1:08**
Reply chips highlighted one at a time: "Please repeat that", "I need a break", "Can you help me".
*Voiceover:* Respond faster with phrases like "Please repeat that" or "I need a break".

**Scene 8 · 1:08–1:18**
Settings with four clearly separated controls: interface, typing, voice, and listening language. Large labels.
*Voiceover:* Ten languages, with separate settings for interface, typing, voice, and listening.

**Scene 9 · 1:18–1:28**
Hero shot with a calm on-device privacy visual — soft glow or shield near the phone. No cloud symbols.
*Voiceover:* Private by default. AI runs on device so personal communication stays with the user.

**Scene 10 · 1:28–1:35**
Polished end card. Phone on a warm gradient. Space for logo and CTA added in post.
*Voiceover:* SpeakEasy — symbols, voice, and on-device AI for everyday communication.
