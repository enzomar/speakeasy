Privacy Policy

Effective date: 13 March 2026

SpeakEasy ("we", "us", "our") is committed to protecting the privacy of every person who uses the application. This policy explains what information we collect, why we collect it, how we use it, and the choices you have.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Account Information

When you create an account we collect your email address and an encrypted password hash. We use Firebase Authentication to manage sign-in securely. We do not store your password in plain text.

1.2 Subscription & Payment Data

Payments are processed entirely by Apple App Store, Google Play, or RevenueCat. We never see or store your credit card number, bank details, or billing address. We receive only a subscription status (active / expired / trial) and an anonymous transaction identifier.

1.3 On-Device Data

Communication history, saved phrases, favourites, board customisations, n-gram learning data, AI context, and text-to-speech settings are stored locally on your device. This data is never uploaded to our servers.

1.4 Crash & Usage Analytics

We may collect anonymous, aggregated crash reports and basic usage metrics (e.g., app opens, feature usage counts) to improve app stability. These contain no personally-identifiable information and cannot be traced back to individual communications.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To create and manage your account and authenticate you securely.
  • To manage your subscription status and free trial entitlement.
  • To send transactional emails (password resets, account changes) — never marketing.
  • To improve app reliability through anonymised diagnostics.

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information. We share data only with:

  • Firebase (Google) — authentication and anonymous crash reporting.
  • RevenueCat — subscription management.
  • Apple / Google — in-app purchase processing.

Each service processes data under its own privacy policy and applicable law.

4. On-Device Processing

SpeakEasy's AI features — sentence prediction, grammar correction, contextual suggestions, and Listen Mode speech recognition — run entirely on your device. Audio from the microphone is processed locally and is never transmitted to an external server.

5. Data Retention

  • Account data is retained while your account is active. When you delete your account, all server-side data (email, auth tokens) is permanently erased.
  • On-device data (history, favourites, settings) remains on your device until you clear it manually or uninstall the app.
  • Subscription records are kept by Apple, Google, and RevenueCat under their respective retention policies.

6. Your Rights (GDPR & Global)

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you.
  • Rectify inaccurate data (e.g., update your email).
  • Delete your account and all server-side data — available directly in the app under Profile → Delete account.
  • Export your on-device data (history and settings) from the app.
  • Withdraw consent at any time by deleting your account.

To exercise any of these rights, email privacy@speakeasyapp.io.

7. Children's Privacy

SpeakEasy may be used by children under the supervision of a parent or caregiver. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 (or the applicable age in your jurisdiction) without parental consent. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, contact us and we will delete it promptly.

8. Security

We protect your data with industry-standard measures including TLS encryption in transit, Firebase's security infrastructure, and keeping sensitive communication data on-device. No system is perfectly secure, but we take every reasonable precaution.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Material changes will be communicated via the app or by email. The "Effective date" at the top reflects the latest revision.

10. Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy or your data, contact us at privacy@speakeasyapp.io.
