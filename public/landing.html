How it works

Built to make communication feel lighter.

Start with symbols, add text when you want, and let SpeakEasy say it out loud. When conversation moves quickly, Listen Mode can help suggest a reply — still on device.

01

Tap symbols or type

Build a sentence with pictograms, words, or both. The interface stays simple, thumb-friendly, and easy to scan.

02

Get gentle support

Instant heuristics and optional AI suggestions help complete common phrases without taking control away from the person using it.

03

Speak clearly

Use text-to-speech with adjustable voice settings, language choices, and saved phrases for the requests you make most often.

04

Stay private

History, preferences, and AI context stay local where it matters most, keeping everyday communication focused on speed, privacy, and ease of use.

What makes it feel different

Designed for real-life AAC, not a crowded dashboard.

SpeakEasy combines a calm interface with practical tools for daily communication, whether the goal is a quick request, a repeated routine, or a more natural conversation.

💬

Calm symbol board

Organised categories, quick-reply tabs, and a mobile-first layout make it easy to reach common phrases without extra friction. Pictograms are sourced from OpenSymbols, including symbols from ARASAAC.

🧠

On-device reply support

Suggestions come from instant heuristics, history-aware predictions, and optional local AI rather than a remote server.

👂

Listen Mode

Hear what someone said, review contextual reply ideas, and choose a response when a live conversation needs extra support.

🌍

Flexible multilingual setup

Keep interface language, typing language, listening language, and text-to-speech language separate for bilingual or multilingual communication.

⭐

History and favourites

Save the phrases that matter, replay them quickly, and keep a local memory of routines, requests, and personal communication patterns.

📱

Made for web and mobile

Use it in the browser, add it to the home screen, or run it through Capacitor on iOS and Android for a more native feel.

Built for real-life moments

Support for daily needs, conversation, and routine communication.

SpeakEasy is designed for people with ALS, autism, aphasia, stroke, cerebral palsy, and anyone who needs an alternative or augmented voice.

It works well for quick requests, care settings, home use, repeated routines, and live conversations where a calm interface matters.

At home

Ask for food, water, medication, help, rest, comfort, or familiar routine phrases.

In care

Share needs clearly, save common requests, and keep communication consistent across caregivers.

In community

Use multilingual voices, fast replies, and listen support for school, work, or public spaces.

Privacy promise

Private by design.

SpeakEasy is built so the important parts stay close to the person using it. Text-to-speech settings, saved phrases, n-gram learning, and on-device AI stay local.

  • Local by default Settings, history, and favourites stay on the device.
  • Offline-friendly Core AAC communication works without needing a constant connection.
  • On-device AI Contextual support is designed to run locally instead of sending every request away.
