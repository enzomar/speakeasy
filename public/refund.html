Refund Policy

Effective date: 13 March 2026

We want you to feel confident in your purchase. This policy explains how cancellations and refunds work for SpeakEasy subscriptions.

1. Free Trial

Every new account starts with a 14-day free trial that includes full access to every feature. You will not be charged during the trial period. If you cancel before the trial ends, you will not be billed at all.

2. Subscription Cancellation

  • You can cancel your subscription at any time through your device's app store settings (Apple App Store or Google Play Store).
  • Cancellation takes effect at the end of the current billing period. You will retain access to all features until that date.
  • We do not pro-rate partial months. Once a billing cycle has started, it runs to completion.

How to cancel

  • iOS: Settings → Apple ID → Subscriptions → SpeakEasy → Cancel Subscription.
  • Android: Google Play Store → Menu → Subscriptions → SpeakEasy → Cancel.

3. Refund Eligibility

Because subscriptions are processed by Apple and Google, refund requests are handled by the respective platform:

  • Apple App Store — Request a refund at reportaproblem.apple.com. Apple reviews each request individually under its own refund policy.
  • Google Play Store — Request a refund via Google Play subscriptions or the Google Play Help Centre. Google reviews each request under its own refund policy.

4. Exceptional Circumstances

If you experience a technical issue that prevents you from using the Service and neither Apple nor Google can resolve it, contact us at support@speakeasyapp.io. We will review the situation on a case-by-case basis and, where appropriate, grant a service credit or assist you in obtaining a refund through the relevant store.

5. Account Deletion

Deleting your SpeakEasy account does not automatically cancel your subscription. Please cancel your subscription through your app store before deleting your account to avoid further charges.

6. Price Changes

If we increase the subscription price, you will be notified at least 30 days before the change takes effect. If you do not wish to continue at the new price, you may cancel before the next renewal.

7. EU Consumer Rights

If you are located in the European Union, you have additional rights under the Consumer Rights Directive. In particular, the 14-day withdrawal right for digital content is satisfied by our 14-day free trial — no payment is taken during that period. If you believe your consumer rights have not been respected, contact us or your local consumer protection authority.

8. Contact

Questions about refunds? Email us at support@speakeasyapp.io.
